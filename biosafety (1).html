Biosafety

The safety conditions of our laboratory environment and of the members is of utmost importance to each of our members. We understand that maintaining biosafety standards is imperative to any procedure performed in the lab. The Wet Lab members conduct experiments in the Project Lab in the Department of Biotechnology, which holds a Biosafety Level 2 designation. The lab adheres to the guidelines outlined by the Indian government and the Centre of Bioethics at Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE).

Safety Practices

Certain practices are strictly prohibited in the lab. This includes eating and drinking, strict application of lab coats, protective goggles and a strict adherence to BSL-2 protocol. Additionally, all laboratory equipment, including centrifuges, autoclaves and spectrophotometers are handled with care. The laboratory is also fumigated every 4 weeks to ensure the standard is met.
